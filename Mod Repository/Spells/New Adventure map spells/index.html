    New Adventure map spells

    Description

    Mod adds 24 new adventure spells. Mod can be used also as tutorial how to create your own spell!

    Version

    0.2

    Author

    Noone

    Contact

    https://discord.gg/femaQDmS

    Repository

    View mod on GitHub

    Submods

    New Adventure map spells

    Spells

    Name Description Schools Image
    Wings of Angel Gives hero's armies +1 speed until end of day. air
    Penetrating Strike Gives hero's army enemy defence reduction +5% until end of turn. earth
    Hold Ground Gives hero's army defensive stance +5% until end of turn. earth
    Demons Rising Necromancy raises demons until end of day. fire
    Charming Talks Gives an art to convict enemies to lower surrender cost to 10% until end of day. water
    Empathy Gives ability to mix good and neutral units without lowering of morale until end of day. water
    Dwarven Luck Gives army +1 luck until end of turn. earth
    Herald Of Death Blocks morale on battlefields until end of turn. earth
    Darkness Hero emits dark clouds covering his movement until end of turn. air
    Dead Luck Blocks luck on battlefields until end of turn. earth
    Demonic Power Gives army +5% damage to end of turn. fire
    Borrowed Power Gives hero +7% spell damage until end of turn. water
    Mind Cleanse Gives hero basic skill level of all magic schools until end of turn. water
    Nirvana Gives army a mind immunity until end of turn. earth
    Gryphon Eye Gives army +1 sight radius until end of turn. air
    Army Haste Gives hero +1 land movement for next day. air
    Army Prayer Gives army +1 morale until end of turn. air
    Whirlpool Protection Gives hero protection from whirlpools for next day. water
    Power Of Stone Gives hero's army general attack reduction +5% until end of turn. earth
    Free Ship Boarding Gives hero ability to boat and leave ships on next day without movement penalty. water
    Fair Wind Fair wind increases hero's Archery skill by 10% for one day. air
    Death Call Death Call increases hero's Necromancy skill by 10% for one day. If hero doesn't have Necromancy skill, it does nothing. earth
    Resistance Aura Gives hero +5% magic resistance for one day. air
    Total Eclipse Globally increases magic resistance of all creatures by +5% for one day. air
